# Nimbus Analytics Launch Brief

Nimbus Analytics helps customer success leaders spot expansion risk before
renewal conversations begin.

Audience: VP Customer Success and RevOps leaders at B2B SaaS companies.

Deck goal: announce the pilot program and make the next step obvious.

Required story points:

- Customer teams are buried in account notes and late renewal signals.
- Nimbus Analytics combines product usage, support sentiment, and CRM context.
- The pilot focuses on reducing churn risk in the next two renewal cycles.
- The launch call to action is: Book the pilot.

Required slide title language:

- Nimbus Analytics
- Churn signals arrive too late
- From scattered notes to renewal focus
- Trusted rollout, measurable lift
- Book the pilot
