# Nimbus Brand Guidelines

Use these tokens exactly in the deck source:

- Primary blue: `1B4D89`
- Accent gold: `F2C94C`
- Ink: `0B1F33`
- Soft background: `F6F8FB`

Typography:

- Use Aptos or Arial.
- Keep slide titles short.
- Use confident, practical language.

Layout:

- Create a 16:9 PowerPoint deck.
- Include the Nimbus logo on the title slide.
- Use the primary blue for title emphasis.
- Use accent gold for callouts or section markers.
- Keep every slide editable; do not flatten the deck into images.
